      Lubumbashi Gold Smelting and Processing Company: Harnessing Africa's Mineral Wealth

      Welcome to Lubumbashi Gold Smelting and Processing Company, headquartered in the heart of Africa, where the earth's riches are as vast and diverse as the continent itself.
