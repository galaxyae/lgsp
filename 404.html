Discover Lubumbashi Gold Smelting and Processing Company: Africa's Gateway to Global Wealth

Welcome to Lubumbashi Gold Smelting and Processing Company, proudly rooted in the heart of Africa, where the earth's treasures abound and our legacy is intertwined with the continent's rich mineral tapestry.
